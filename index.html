Fully Explained Future Plans

  • My ultimate goal is to become a Red Teamer, while also gaining solid experience as a Pentester along the way.

  • I plan to get hands-on experience in Red Teaming by joining a team as a paid/unpaid intern, since real-world exposure is the best way to learn.

  • After that, I plan to pursue eJPT, which is a great entry-level hands-on certification.

  • Next, I’ll challenge myself with PNPT, to get closer to real-world penetration testing experience.

  • By the time I graduate, I aim to complete either OSCP or CRTP, depending on where I want to specialize more (general pentesting vs Active Directory). If I get OSCP, I will get CRTP later and vice versa.

  • Once I gain some professional experience, I’ll move to advanced certifications such as OSEP or CRTE.

  • Later, when I’ve grown as a Red Teamer, I will pursue CRTO, which focuses on adversary emulation and C2 operations.

  • Alongside certifications, I also plan to attend community events like DEF CON, to learn from experts and network with professionals.
